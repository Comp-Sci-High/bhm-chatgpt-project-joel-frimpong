About Black Histoy & Spider-Man

Black History matters in recognizing the struggles, accomplishments, and triumphs of Black individuals, diversifying and equalizing society. Miles Morales, as a Black and Puerto Rican Spider-Man, provides representation and role models for young Black individuals, providing them with a superhero who is representative of their own identities and struggles, highlighting the importance of diversity in media.
